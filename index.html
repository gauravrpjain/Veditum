Who We Are

We are a group of five passionate students from IIT Kharagpur committed to bringing about a change in the society at the global level. Our team has been chosen as the only team from India for the finals at San Francisco out of more than 1000s of teams from Ivy league institutions to participate in the 2015 Hult Prize Competition, a startup accelerator for Social Entrepreneurship. This year's problem statement is on the Early Childhood Education in Urban Slums and has been specially drafted by President Clinton with a special call to action and is also a focus of the Millennium Development goals of the UN.

What We Believe

Veditum, in Sanskrit means ‘To understand’, ‘To Know’.
We believe children of the age group 0-6 need a positive, community-integrated environment to, learn and develop. Thus, we aim to create a solution that is sustainable and emerges from within the community, while keeping in mind the diversity and cultural differences in different regions to make the plan scalable, sustainable and sensitive for children to develop and grow.

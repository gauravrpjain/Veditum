The Team

We are an enthusiastic team from diverse backgrounds with the passion and skills to jointly conceive a plan to be the game changers in this field. With expertise in technology, architecture, entrepreneurship, education along with a zeal for social change, spiced with the right amount of madness, we are inspired to take up the challenge for the greater good. From our experiences and efforts, we have been exposed to the scenario in urban slums and we understand the challenges and complexities of life and its necessities therein.

Abhimanyu Abrol

Fianl Year, Architecture

Abhimanyu’s experience as founder of Fundacja Aravindam (aravindam.org), an NGO committed to facilitate development of education and environment for the underprivileged youth and children is our bedrock. He is a final year student majoring in Architechure and Regional Planning from IIT Kharagpur.

Pooja Rai

Final Year, Architecture

Pooja has authored a book on women entrepreneurs- “The Road Not Taken”. She has gained valuable insight into the various aspects of entrepreneurial journey by personal case studies. By conducting the Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive, she inspired many young entrepreneurs. She is a final year student majoring in Architechure and Regional Planning from IIT Kharagpur.

Siddharth Agrawal

Fianl Year, Aerospace

Siddharth’s tireless initiative for the cause of child rights and grassroots level entrepreneurs through his cross country cycling expedition across India dictates his intrinsic zeal for the cause of change. He has cycled from Kolkata to Mumbai (2500 kms) and raised 1.6 lakhs for child education. He is a final year student majoring in Aerospace Engineering from IIT Kharagpur.

Vishesh Gupta

Fianl Year, Architecture

Vishesh volunteered with Urban Community Labs, SELCO Foundation, to redesign the slum dwelling unit, and has learnt about various problems faced by spending long hours within the community. He is a final year student majoring in Architechure and Regional Planning from IIT Kharagpur.

Gaurav Jain

Pre Final Year, Metallurgy and Embedded Systems


Technology as a domain has always interested Gaurav. Having a strong technical and academic background, he has been involved with various development activities including Mobile Application development, Web development and product developments. He is a Mozillian and his experience as the Google ambassador from IIT Kharagpur enhances our grip on the technological aspects of the solution. He is a pre-final year student majoring in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from IIT Kharagpur.
